Qubee Gets Pricey

July 8th, 2010

Just one month after the official launch of the WiMAX product, Qubee has increased its prices recently. While I was still figuring out the reason for such low volume packages that are offered by Qubee, the rise in price is something I am totally unable to understand.



With a very few customer base, Qubee still have a way to go to survive in the wireless broadband market and co-exist with giants like Wateen. As to my experience, the ISP has so far failed to provide wide coverage with good browsing speed and still haven’t heard of any positive remarks from any of my friends who have used it. My point is not to criticize the broadband provider, but this increase in price is somewhat incomprehensible to me.



If you have received a telephone call from a telemarketing executive trying to convince you, there are 50 percent chances that you will not be convinced with PTCL or cable modem already working hip at your place. As I got to hear, only 20 percent of all the sales that is made over calls make it to the deployment whereas the 50 percent change their decisions before deployment after agreeing on the call previously. The rest percentage doesn’t get to the customer end due to technical reasons.

  2. Absolutely no sense of age ratings. It completely surprises me how a movie can be suitable for a 10 year old and 30 year old at the same time? Any movie which is made exclusively for kids (take for example Koi Mil Gya and likewise), ends up having cheesy romantic songs in the rain and despicable dialogues every now and then. Or something which could be entertaining for a kid in early teens (03 Idiots probably), ends up showing intricate details of how a woman should deliver a baby with bunch of guys around!
  3. The Masala factor, which movie should I go for if I want to watch some action? And what is the right movie to entertain me if I need some laughs? Don’t worry; they have all in one package! One movie can easily contain all the romance, action, violence, drama and comedy to suit your best mood! For me, its blending onions and chilies with my sweet mango shake! Yukh!

They all seem to have the same theme.
