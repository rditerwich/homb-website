The smart and green way of city transportation

Hop on my Bike is an app to match people looking for a ride on the back of a bike and riders who offer a ride on the back of their bike. The app not only provides easy search, it also shows reviews of both riders and passengers and makes it easy for passengers to pay the rider without any cash in their pocket

 

What is it?

Hop on my Bike is an app to match people looking for a ride on the back of a bike and riders who offer a ride

 

Why?

Hop on my Bike is the green answer to city transportation challenges. Many cities face problems with heavy million people.
